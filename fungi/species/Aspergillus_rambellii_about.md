About *Aspergillus rambellii* 
-----------------------------



***Aspergillus rambellii*** is a species of fungus in the family
Trichocomaceae. It accumulates very large amounts of sterigmatocystin,
3-O-methylsterigmatocystin and aflatoxin B1.
