About *Talaromyces islandicus* 
------------------------------



***Penicillium islandicum*** is an anamorph species of the genus of
*Penicillium* which produces luteoskyrin, simatoxin, cyclochlorotine,
rugulosin, islanditoxin and chitosanase.
