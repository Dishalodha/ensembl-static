About *Serpula lacrymans var. lacrymans S7.3 (GCA\_000218725)* 
--------------------------------------------------------------



***Serpula lacrymans*** is one of the fungi that cause damage to timber
referred to as dry rot. It is a basidiomycete in the order Boletales.
