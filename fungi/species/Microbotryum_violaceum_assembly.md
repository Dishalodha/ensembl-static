The genomic assembly used comes from the [*Microbotryum violaceum*
Sequencing
Project](http://www.broadinstitute.org/annotation/genome/Microbotryum_violaceum/MultiHome.html),
[Broad Institute of Harvard and MIT](http://www.broadinstitute.org/),
using genomic DNA from Mike Perlin at the University of Louisville,
Kentucky, as described
[here](http://www.broadinstitute.org/annotation/genome/Microbotryum_violaceum/MultiHome.html),
and deposited in INSDC as Genome Assembly
[GCA\_000166175](http://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/GCA_000166175) (WGS
project [AEIJ1000000](http://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/AEIJ00000000)).
