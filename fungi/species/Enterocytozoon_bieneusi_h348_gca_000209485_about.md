About *Enterocytozoon bieneusi H348 (GCA\_000209485)* 
-----------------------------------------------------



***Enterocytozoon bieneusi*** is a species of the order Microsporidia
which infects the intestinal epithelial cells. It is an obligate
intracellular parasite.
