About *Macrophomina phaseolina MS6* 
-----------------------------------



***Macrophomina phaseolina*** is a Botryosphaeriaceae fungus that is a
plant pathogen that causes charcoal rot on many plant species including
*Zea mays* and *Pinus elliottii*.
