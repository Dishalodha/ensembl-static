**About *Sipha flava***
-------------------------
*Sipha flava* is a species of aphid in the family Aphididae. It is 
native to North America. Its common name is yellow sugarcane aphid 
(*Sipha flava*).

Taxonomy ID [143950](https://www.uniprot.org/taxonomy/143950)
