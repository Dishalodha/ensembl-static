**About *Penaeus monodon***
-------------------------
*Penaeus monodon*, commonly known as the giant tiger prawn, Asian 
tiger shrimp, black tiger shrimp (*Penaeus monodon*), and other names, 
is a marine crustacean that is widely reared for food.
