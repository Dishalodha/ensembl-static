About *Strongyloides ratti*
---------------------------

The nematode *Strongyloides ratti*, or rat threadworm, is a minute
common gastro-intestinal parasite of the rat. *S. ratti* is a laboratory
analogue of the human parasite, *S. stercoralis*.
