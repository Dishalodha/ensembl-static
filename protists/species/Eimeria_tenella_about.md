[![Wikipedia](/img/wikipedia_logo_v2_en.png){#wiki_icon}](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eimeria_tenella)

***Eimeria tenella*** is a species of *Eimeria* that causes hemorrhagic
cecal coccidiosis in young poultry.
