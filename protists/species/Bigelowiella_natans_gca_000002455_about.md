[![Wikipedia](/img/wikipedia_logo_v2_en.png){#wiki_icon}](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bigelowiella_natans)

***Bigelowiella natans*** is a species of Chlorarachniophyte alga that
is a model organism for the Rhizaria.
