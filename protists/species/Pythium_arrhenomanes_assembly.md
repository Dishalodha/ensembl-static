Assembly
--------

The genome is \~45Mb and are assembled into 10,978 saffolds. The
assembly for the genome was provided by [Pythium Genome
Database](http://pythium.plantbiology.msu.edu/) and is also available
from the EMBL/Genbank/DDBJ databases under the accession
[GCA\_000387505.2](http://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/GCA_000387505.2)
